\caption{System architecture (main technical pipeline). The top row
produces two independently reusable assets: an unsupervised
\textbf{structural metric} $\branchCoherence$ (capturing the
structural pattern of benign process lifecycles) and a
self-supervised \textbf{semantic Transformer} (learning the causal
distribution of benign events); the two remain fully independent of
each other throughout. The bottom row is the main pipeline: seed
scoring first flags high-loss edges as suspicious anchors;
\textbf{bidirectional extraction} grows the search from each anchor
simultaneously forward and backward via an \textbf{undirected,
meet-in-the-middle} search, so each side only needs to expand about
half the hop budget before the two frontiers meet, while a
\textbf{hub-degree guard} prevents combinatorial blow-up at
high-fan-in transit nodes; connectivity is then decided by a
\textbf{3-tier ladder} -- causal direction $\to$ info-flow $\to$
structural (weak evidence) -- reusing $\branchCoherence$ to guide the
decision -- yielding the dependency-plausible chains. The Transformer
reuses its trained weights to score each chain as $A(\chain)$, and
chains exceeding threshold $\theta$ are merged into attack paths.}
