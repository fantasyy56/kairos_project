\caption{System architecture.
\textbf{Offline} (top): from benign audit logs we (i) draw
process-lifecycle anchors and run the calibration protocol to obtain
the structural metric $\branchCoherence$, and (ii) mine benign
chains under the calibrated extractor to train the self-supervised
Transformer (with per-component eCDFs and threshold $\theta$).
\textbf{Online} (bottom): for each test day, the seed scorer flags
high-loss edges that anchor bidirectional chain extraction guided by
$\branchCoherence$; the resulting dependency-plausible chains
are scored by the Transformer to yield $A(\chain)$, and chains with
$A(\chain){>}\theta$ are merged into attack paths.
The two layers---structural coherence ($\branchCoherence$,
blue) and semantic anomaly ($A$, red)---are kept on independent
foundations throughout (\S\ref{sec:design}).}
\label{fig:architecture}
